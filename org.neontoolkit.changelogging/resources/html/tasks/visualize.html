Changes can be visualized from the "Change Log View" provided by the plug-in (Figure4). From this view, users can visualize the ontologies that are being logged and for each of them, the history of changes sorted in chronological order. The list of changes is displayed in a table that shows the most relevant information (e.g. author of the change, time of the change, type of the change, etc.) and when a change is selected the complete information is displayed in the interface.
